Avocado Toast Recipe Ingredients: 2 slices of whole grain bread 1 ripe avocado Juice of 1/2 a lemon Salt and pepper, to taste Optional toppings: cherry tomatoes, radish slices, or a poached egg Instructions: Toast the Bread: Toast the slices of bread to your desired level of crispness in a toaster or on a skillet. Prepare the Avocado: While the bread is toasting, peel and pit the avocado. In a small bowl, mash the avocado with a fork. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper, and stir to combine. Assemble the Toast: Spread the mashed avocado evenly over the toasted bread slices. Add Toppings (Optional): Top with sliced cherry tomatoes, radishes, or a poached egg for added flavor and nutrients. Serve: Enjoy your avocado toast immediately for the best taste and texture. Nutritional Notes: Whole Grain Bread: Provides a good source of fiber which helps with digestion and promotes a feeling of fullness. Avocado: Rich in monounsaturated fats, which are heart-healthy fats. Avocados are also a great source of vitamins E, C, and K, and have more potassium than bananas. Lemon Juice: Adds a boost of vitamin C, important for immune function and skin health. Optional Toppings: Cherry Tomatoes: Provide additional vitamins A and C, and lycopene, an antioxidant linked with reduced risk of certain cancers. Radish: Offers a spicy flavor and is a good source of antioxidants. Poached Egg: Adds high-quality protein and vitamins D, B12, and minerals such as selenium and zinc.
