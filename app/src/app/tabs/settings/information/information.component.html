Information & Sources

We are committed to providing accurate and reliable nutritional information in our meal plans. Below, you will find the primary sources of data and guidelines used in the app. We encourage users to review these resources for more detailed information.

1. Australian Food Standards

Our meal plans and ingredient information are aligned with the guidelines and standards provided by Food Standards Australia New Zealand (FSANZ). FSANZ is a statutory authority responsible for developing food standards for Australia and New Zealand. You can learn more about their regulations and guidelines here:

  • Food Standards Australia New Zealand (FSANZ) - Official Website

2. Nutritional Databases

We use reliable nutritional databases to ensure the accuracy of the nutritional information provided for each meal. These sources are recognized for their comprehensive food composition data:

  • AFCD, previously known as NUTTAB
  • Australian Food Composition Database
